SOLAS Compliance

VGM Certificate

Safety of Life at Sea (SOLAS) container weight declarations.

Verified Gross Mass (VGM) = Cargo Weight + Packaging Weight + Pallet Weight + Container Tare Weight

The system inherits weights from the Packing List and merges them with the physical Container Tare registers to print legal weighbridge certificates directly.

Infrastructure & Security

Backup & Restore Management

An enterprise-grade disaster recovery and data archival system built strictly to ERP standards.

Administrative Restriction: Access to manual trigger commands, auto-backup settings, and restore tools is restricted strictly to users assigned to the super_admin, company_admin, or company_owner roles.

System Architecture:

  • Manual Backup: Generates a full archive instantly, grouping a binary export of the primary SQL database (pg_dump format) and all stored files (company logos, product catalog images, shipping bill PDFs) in the /uploads directory into a single encrypted .zip package.
  • Automated Weekly Scheduler: Governed by a system background daemon (node-cron), automated weekly snapshots are executed at 2:00 AM on Sundays. The retention mechanism automatically cleans up older records based on the configured policy count.
  • Safe System Restore: To prevent data loss, the restore engine automatically executes an immutable safety snapshot (pre_restore_snapshot) before applying the selected ZIP archive, allowing database rollback in the event of an operational issue.
// Automated cron execution schedule mapping Daily: "0 2 * * *" (Executes at 2:00 AM every morning) Weekly: "0 2 * * 0" (Executes at 2:00 AM every Sunday - Default) Monthly: "0 2 1 * *" (Executes at 2:00 AM on the 1st of every month)
