# 🌐 Tile Exporter ERP - Website Architecture & Content Strategy

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Executive Summary:** This document outlines the structural, strategic, and content architecture for the public-facing marketing website of **Tile Exporter ERP**. The website is engineered to be a high-performance B2B conversion engine targeting global Ceramic, Tile, and Sanitaryware exporters.

---

## 1. Primary Website Goals

The primary objective of this website is to operate as a high-velocity conversion pipeline. Every element, from typography to micro-animations, is designed to convert visitors into active leads:

*   **Demo Bookings:** Primary CTA for enterprise clients, large export houses, and manufacturers.
*   **Trial Activations:** Secondary CTA for mid-sized traders seeking immediate, frictionless value.
*   **Customer Acquisition:** Ultimate goal driven by transparent value propositions, robust security claims, and seamless onboarding.

---

## 2. Homepage Architecture

### 2.1 High-Impact Hero Section

> [!TIP]
> The Hero section must immediately communicate authority and specific industry relevance. Avoid generic SaaS copy.

*   **Headline:** The Operating System for Modern Ceramic & Sanitaryware Exporters.
*   **Subheadline:** Automate export documentation, eliminate manual errors, and scale your global shipments with the industry's only purpose-built ERP.
*   **Primary CTA:** `Book a Demo` (Solid, high-contrast brand color)
*   **Secondary CTA:** `Start Free Trial` (Elegant outline button)
*   **Tertiary CTA:** `Watch 2-Min Overview` (Play icon with a subtle hover pulse)
*   **Trust Banner:** "Trusted by 500+ Export Houses Globally" (Including grayscale logos of CAPEXIL, FIEO, and top industry players).

### 2.2 The "Status Quo" Problem Section

**"Exporting shouldn't be this hard."**
Highlight the painful, expensive reality of the traditional export industry:

*   **Manual Data Entry:** Wasting hours copying data across disconnected spreadsheets.
*   **Compliance Errors:** Costly mistakes leading to customs holds, demurrage charges, and VGM fines.
*   **Shipment Delays:** Missed vessel cut-offs due to inefficient, siloed workflows.
*   **Zero Auditability:** No accountability for document changes, approvals, or internal communication.

### 2.3 The Automated Solution Engine

> [!NOTE]
> **"Meet Tile Exporter ERP: Your Complete Export Workflow, Automated."**

Provide a clean, animated pipeline graphic showing data flowing seamlessly:
`Inquiry ➔ Proforma Invoice ➔ Proforma Order ➔ Export Invoice ➔ Packing List ➔ Annexure ➔ VGM ➔ Shipping Instructions`
*Message: Enter data once, generate all compliance documents instantly.*

### 2.4 Deep-Dive Feature Showcase

Showcase the comprehensive module suite with real business impact:

*   **Multi-Tenant Business Management:** Manage multi-company profiles and bank details centrally. *(Scale operations without administrative overhead.)*
*   **CRM & Lead Pipeline:** Track inquiries, send quotations, and close deals faster. *(Higher win rates and organized sales pipelines.)*
*   **Intelligent Product Catalogue:** Centralized master data handling complex tile (SQM/Pallets) and sanitaryware (Pieces) calculations.
*   **Quality Control (QC):** Mobile-friendly quality inspection reports with live image uploads. *(Ensure zero defects before container stuffing.)*
*   **Export Documentation Automation:** One-click generation of complete pre- and post-shipment sets. *(Save 4+ hours per shipment.)*
*   **Finance & Forex:** Track advances, balances, and commission payments with automated currency conversion.
*   **Enterprise Administration:** 
    *   Real-time system notifications
    *   Irreversible document locking
    *   Strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
    *   Immutable Audit Trails

### 2.5 Industry-Specific Workflows

**"Built specifically for your business model."**

*   **Ceramic Manufacturers:** Streamline production-to-export workflows.
*   **Sanitaryware Manufacturers:** Handle complex SKUs and volumetric weight calculations effortlessly.
*   **Export Houses & Traders:** Manage multiple buyer portfolios and seamless back-to-back documentation.

## 6. Technical & SEO Foundation

> [!NOTE]
> The website must be a technical masterpiece to reflect the quality of the underlying ERP software.

*   **Framework:** Built using Next.js for optimal Server-Side Rendering (SSR) and SEO performance.
*   **Core Web Vitals:** Guaranteed LCP < 2.5s and seamless mobile responsiveness.
*   **Analytics Stack:** Google Analytics 4, PostHog (for product analytics), and Hotjar.
*   **CRM Webhooks:** Seamless flow of leads directly into HubSpot/Salesforce.
*   **Target Keywords:** Tile Export ERP, Export Documentation Software, Ceramic Export Software, VGM Software, Automated Export Documentation.
